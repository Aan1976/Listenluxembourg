Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At ParlerLUX, we take your privacy and the confidentiality of your conversations very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Preferences and conversation topics
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type)
  • Conversation metadata (time, duration) — never the content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our companionship matching service
  • Process payments and bookings
  • Ensure platform safety and screening
  • Communicate important updates

3. Confidentiality of Conversations

All conversations on ParlerLUX are strictly private and confidential. We do not record, store, or share the content of your conversations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe)
  • Companions (only necessary profile information)
  • Legal authorities (if required by Luxembourg law)

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in Luxembourg/EU, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the CNPD (Luxembourg data protection authority)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@parlerlux.com
