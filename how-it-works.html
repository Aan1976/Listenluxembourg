How Listen Works

Four quiet steps to meaningful conversation

01

Tell us what you're looking for

Answer a few gentle questions about your preferences, topics you’d like to discuss, and what kind of conversation you need. Skip anything you want.

02

Choose your companion

Browse thoughtfully selected profiles. See their background, languages, and availability. Take your time — no pressure.

03

Have the conversation

Connect via secure video call, voice call, or meet in person (coffee in Kirchberg, walk in Petrusse, etc). All conversations are strictly platonic.

04

Reflect & Rebook

After the session, leave optional private feedback. Rebook the same companion easily whenever you want.

All conversations are 100% confidential • Companions are carefully screened • Strictly platonic environment
